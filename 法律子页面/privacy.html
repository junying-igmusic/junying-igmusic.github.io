隱私政策

    1. 關於我們

    炎上音樂科技有限公司（以下簡稱「本公司」、「我們」）是一家在香港成立的科技公司，致力於為全球古典音樂社群提供數碼化解決方案。本公司運營的網站為 igmusictech.com（以下簡稱「本網站」）。

    本隱私政策說明我們如何收集、使用、儲存及保護您在使用本網站時所提供的個人資料，適用於全球所有訪客。

    2. 我們收集的資料

    我們僅在您主動提供的情況下收集個人資料，具體如下：

    • 聯繫表單：姓名（選填）、電子郵件地址、查詢主題、標題（選填）、訊息內容。
    • 試用申請表單：電子郵件地址，以及您自願提供的個人簡介（用於評估試用資格）。
    • 瀏覽器本地儲存：我們使用 localStorage 記住您的語言偏好（繁體中文或英語）；使用 sessionStorage 記錄當次瀏覽是否為首次訪問（僅用於控制頁面載入動畫）。以上資料均存儲於您的設備本地，不會傳輸至我們的伺服器。

    我們的網站不使用 Cookie、不部署任何網絡追蹤工具（如 Google Analytics）、不進行廣告追蹤。

    3. 我們如何使用您的資料

    我們使用所收集的個人資料用於以下目的：

    • 回覆查詢：處理您通過聯繫表單提交的查詢或合作意向。
    • 試用資格審核：評估並通知試用申請結果。
    • 產品通知及行銷：向申請試用的用戶發送產品上線通知、版本更新及相關行銷訊息。您可隨時通過郵件底部的退訂連結或直接聯繫我們取消訂閱。

    我們不會將您的個人資料出售、出租或以其他方式轉讓給任何第三方用於行銷目的。

    4. 第三方服務

    我們使用 Web3Forms（web3forms.com）作為表單提交的中間處理服務。當您提交聯繫表單或試用申請時，您的資料將通過 Web3Forms 的伺服器轉發至我們的電子郵箱，之後由我們在 Google Workspace 企業郵箱中保存及處理。

    Web3Forms 僅作資料傳輸用途，不會永久儲存您的個人資料。如需了解其數據處理方式，請參閱 Web3Forms 隱私政策。

    除上述服務外，本網站不接入任何其他第三方數據處理服務。

    5. 資料儲存與安全

    您提交的個人資料由我們通過 Google Workspace 企業郵箱接收並儲存，Google Workspace 採用業界標準的加密與安全措施。我們的資料存儲地點主要位於香港，並可能經由 Google 的全球基礎設施進行處理。

    我們目前對電子郵件中的個人資料未設定自動刪除機制，資料將按業務需要保留。若您希望我們刪除您的資料，請通過下方聯繫方式提出要求，我們將在合理時間內予以處理。

    6. 國際資料傳輸

    本公司設立於香港。當您從歐洲經濟區（EEA）、英國或其他司法管轄區訪問本網站並提交資料時，您的個人資料可能會被傳輸至香港及 Web3Forms、Google 所在的其他地區進行處理。

    香港目前獲歐盟認定為具備「充分」個人資料保護水平的地區，因此此類跨境傳輸符合 GDPR 的相關規定。

    7. 您的權利

    根據您所在地區的適用法律，您可能享有以下權利：

    • 查閱權：要求查閱我們持有的關於您的個人資料。
    • 更正權：要求更正不準確或不完整的資料。
    • 刪除權：要求刪除您的個人資料（「被遺忘權」）。
    • 限制處理權：在特定情況下要求限制我們處理您的資料。
    • 資料可攜權：要求以結構化、通用格式獲取您的資料。
    • 反對權：反對我們基於合法利益對您的資料進行處理。
    • 撤回同意：若您曾同意我們發送行銷郵件，可隨時撤回該同意。

    如需行使上述任何權利，請通過 info@igmusictech.com 與我們聯繫。我們將在收到請求後的 30 天內予以回應。

    若您認為我們對您個人資料的處理違反了適用法律，您亦有權向相關監管機構提出投訴（如香港個人資料私隱專員公署，或您所在 EEA 成員國的數據保護機構）。

    8. 兒童隱私

    本網站不針對 13 歲以下兒童。我們不會故意收集兒童的個人資料。若您認為我們無意中收集了兒童的資料，請立即通過下方聯繫方式告知我們。

    9. 本政策的變更

    我們可能會不時更新本隱私政策。任何重大變更將通過更新本頁頂部的「最後更新」日期予以反映。建議您定期查閱本政策以了解最新內容。繼續使用本網站即表示您接受更新後的政策。

    10. 聯繫我們

    如您對本隱私政策有任何疑問，或希望行使您的資料權利，請通過以下方式與我們聯繫：

    1. About Us

    Ignition Musictech Limited ("we", "us", "our") is a technology company incorporated in Hong Kong, dedicated to providing digital solutions for the global classical music community. We operate the website igmusictech.com (the "Website").

    This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal data when you use our Website. It applies to all visitors worldwide.

    2. Data We Collect

    We only collect personal data that you actively provide to us:

    • Contact form: Name (optional), email address, inquiry topic, subject (optional), and message.
    • Trial application form: Email address and a voluntary self-introduction used to assess trial eligibility.
    • Browser local storage: We use localStorage to remember your language preference (Traditional Chinese or English), and sessionStorage to record whether you have visited during the current session (used solely to control the page loading animation). This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

    Our Website does not use cookies, does not deploy any web analytics or tracking tools (such as Google Analytics), and does not engage in advertising tracking.

    3. How We Use Your Data

    We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

    • Responding to enquiries: To process and respond to messages submitted via our contact form.
    • Trial eligibility assessment: To evaluate trial applications and notify applicants of the outcome.
    • Product notifications and marketing: To send trial users product launch announcements, update notifications, and related marketing communications. You may unsubscribe at any time via the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us directly.

    We do not sell, rent or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

    4. Third-Party Services

    We use Web3Forms (web3forms.com) as an intermediary service for form submissions. When you submit a contact form or trial application, your data is relayed through Web3Forms' servers to our email inbox, where it is then stored and processed within our Google Workspace environment.

    Web3Forms is used solely for data transmission and does not permanently retain your personal data. For more information on how Web3Forms handles data, please refer to the Web3Forms Privacy Policy.

    Beyond the above, our Website does not integrate any other third-party data processing services.

    5. Data Storage and Security

    Personal data you submit is received and stored via our Google Workspace corporate email accounts, which employ industry-standard encryption and security measures. Our primary data storage is located in Hong Kong, and data may be processed through Google's global infrastructure.

    We do not currently apply an automatic deletion schedule to personal data held in email. Data is retained as long as it remains relevant to business operations. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us using the details below and we will action your request within a reasonable timeframe.

    6. International Data Transfers

    Our company is based in Hong Kong. If you access our Website and submit data from the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions, your personal data may be transferred to Hong Kong and processed in other regions where Web3Forms and Google operate.

    Hong Kong has been recognised by the European Union as providing an adequate level of personal data protection, meaning such transfers are consistent with GDPR requirements.

    7. Your Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under applicable data protection law:

    • Right of access: To request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
    • Right to rectification: To request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
    • Right to erasure: To request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
    • Right to restriction: To request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
    • Right to data portability: To receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
    • Right to object: To object to our processing of your data where we rely on legitimate interests.
    • Right to withdraw consent: If you have consented to receive marketing emails, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@igmusictech.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days of receipt.

    If you believe our processing of your personal data violates applicable law, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority — such as the Office of the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data in Hong Kong, or the data protection authority in your EEA member state.

    8. Children's Privacy

    Our Website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately using the details below.

    9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

    10. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:
